Who We Are

Welcome to the Light of Jesus Pastoral Care. We are one of the Feast Mercy Ministries, under the Light of Jesus Family (founded by Bro. Bo Sanchez). Our mission is to journey with the burdened and distressed through psycho-spiritual care and support, and prayer intercessions.

The Light of Jesus Pastoral Care Center is a mercy ministry founded by Bro. Bo Sanchez in 1993, of the Light of Jesus Family. Our mission is to nurture and empower people through pastoral care. We share Christ's light to those going through life crisis, and providing care that can help transform lives.

It all began with a vision to care for God's hurting people. The dream was to set up a ministry that would share God's light to the lost, the broken, the emotionally and the spiritually wounded.

Under the leadership of Bro Bo Sanchez, founder of the Light of Jesus Family, and six other volunteers — namely, Ditas and Vic Espanol, Cristy Galang, Avic Achaval, Babes Fontillas, and Janet Ealdama (+) — the Light of Jesus Counseling Center was born in July 1993. The group's first project was to set up a telephone hotline operated three hours once a week for people who needed a listening ear.

Today, it continues to pursue that dream of sharing Christ through caring. Now called Light of Jesus Pastoral Care Center (LOJPCC), its trained volunteer Pastoral Carers provide quality care and psycho-spiritual support to people going through rough times through telephone, email, online chat, and face-to-face sessions.

While its operations have been affected by the pandemic, our Pastoral Carers work from home and continue to be compassionate companions journeying with those who need someone to walk with them during these trying times.

In addition, the center offers psycho-spiritual-educational learning sessions that deal with life issues such as depression & suicide, grief & loss, inner healing, and other personal growth retreats.

What We Do

LOJ Pastoral Care provides Psycho-Spiritual Support for individuals going through difficulties. Volunteers are trained in Psychological First Aid.

Our sessions are focused on helping the person stabilize their emotional state, facilitate some insights to better handle the situation and see the Light of Jesus amidst their concerns. Our Care Sessions do not include or replace psychological diagnosis or treatment.
