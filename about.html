Our Journey

Our History

It all began with a vision to care for God's hurting people. The dream was to set up a ministry that would share God's light to the lost, the broken, the emotionally and the spiritually wounded.

Under the leadership of Bro Bo Sanchez, founder of the Light of Jesus Family, and six other volunteers — namely, Ditas and Vic Espanol, Cristy Galang, Avic Achaval, Babes Fontillas, and Janet Ealdama (+) — the Light of Jesus Counseling Center was born in July 1993. The group's first project was to set up a telephone hotline to be operated three hours once a week for people who needed a listening ear.

Today, it continues to pursue that dream of sharing Christ through caring. Now called Light of Jesus Pastoral Care Center (LOJPCC), its trained volunteer Pastoral Carers provide quality care and psycho-spiritual support to people going through rough times. This is done through various mediums — telephone, email, online chat support, and face-to-face sessions.

While its operations have been affected by the pandemic, our Pastoral Carers work from home and continue to be compassionate companions journeying with those who need someone to walk with them during these trying times.

In addition, the center offers psycho-spiritual-educational learning sessions that deal with life issues such as depression & suicide, grief & loss, inner healing, and other personal growth retreats.

Looking back through the years, the LOJPCC has been…

  • Bringing hope to those who are hopeless
  • Being Jesus to those who have lost their way to Him
  • Nurturing and caring for those who are discouraged
  • Touching and inspiring lives
  • Empowering people to love and serve God
  • Equipping those who give care to others

Our Purpose

Vision & Mission

Vision Statement

A compassionate, Christ-centered community where every wounded soul finds hope, healing, and renewed purpose through authentic care, connection, and faith.

Mission Statement

To provide accessible and compassionate psycho-spiritual support by listening deeply, journeying with individuals in their struggles, and guiding them toward healing, resilience, and a deeper encounter with God's love — through care sessions, prayer, formation, and meaningful connections.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

🕊️ Compassion

We meet every person with empathy and genuine care, reflecting Christ's unconditional love for all who are hurting.

🔒 Confidentiality

Everything shared with our Pastoral Carers stays within the bounds of our sessions. Trust is the foundation of care.

✝️ Christ-Centered

Our care is rooted in faith. We journey with individuals toward a deeper encounter with God's healing love.

🤲 Accessibility

All our services are free. We believe that no one should be without support because of financial limitations.

📖 Formation

We continuously train and form our volunteer Pastoral Carers to offer quality, professional, and faith-filled care.

🌱 Empowerment

Our goal is not dependency but growth — to help people rediscover their strength and resilience in Christ.
